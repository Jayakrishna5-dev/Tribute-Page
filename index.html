AWARDS

Academy Award

-RDJ won his first Academy Award for Best Supporting Actor for his portrayal of Lewis Strauss in Oppenheimer (2023).

Golden Globe

-RDJ has won three Golden Globes, including Best Actor in a Motion Picture - Musical or Comedy in 2010 and Best Supporting Actor in a Series, Limited Series, or Motion Picture Made for Television in 2001.

BAFTA

-RDJ won a BAFTA Film Award for Best Supporting Actor for his role in Oppenheimer (2023). He was also nominated for a BAFTA Film Award for Best Supporting Actor in 2009 for Tropic Thunder.

Saturn Award

-RDJ won Saturn Awards for Best Actor in Avengers: Endgame (2019) and Iron Man 3 (2014). He was also nominated for Saturn Awards for Best Actor in Iron Man 2 (2011) and Sherlock Holmes (2010).

Critics Choice Award

-RDJ won a Critics Choice Award for Best Supporting Actor for his role in Oppenheimer (2023). He was also nominated for Critics Choice Awards for Best Actor in an Action Movie in 2013 for The Avengers and 2014 for Iron Man 3.
